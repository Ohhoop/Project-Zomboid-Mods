title=Quick Restart
description=Adds a Quick Restart panel on the death screen that gets you back into the game significantly faster than the normal process.
description=
description=Two options: Restart in a fresh world or respawn in the same one, both restoring your original character.
description=
description=Character is saved upon CREATION and reapplied automatically:
description=- Name, gender, profession
description=- Traits and skills
description=- Full appearance: hair, beard, hair color, skin tone, body hair, hair stubble, beard stubble
description=- All worn clothing with colors and texture variants
description=- Voice type, pitch, and prefix
description=- Spawn region
description=
description=The sandbox settings are captured automatically shortly after spawning. If they have changed since the world was created, the Fresh World button will prompt you to choose between the original creation settings and the current ones.
description=
description=If you choose to "Continue with a new character" instead of using Quick Restart, that new character will become the new original that gets reloaded on the next Quick Restart.
description=
description=If this mod is enabled mid-save, the Quick Restart buttons will be disabled since no character data was saved, as it is saved on character creation. You can "Continue with a new character" if you want to use the Fresh World button to specifically bring over your sandbox settings, for example.
description=
description==========================================================================
description=
description=I did not test it with any other mods, please let me know if this causes any type of issue. I don't expect any, but if the saving part can't catch a modded item for example, it won't be reapplied on Quick Restart.
description=
description=Fresh world Quick Restart is single-player only. (You don't want to delete the world on which everyone else is playing!... Right?)
description=Same world Quick Restart in MP is available but still WIP, please let me know of any issues you encounter.
description=
description=Feel free to provide any feedback you may have in the comment section.
